Overview

Virat Kohli,born on November 5,1988,in Delhi,is one of the world's leading cricketers.He is known for his aggressive batting and strong leadership as the captain of the Indian cricket team.

Kohli has consistently ranked aming the world's top batsman and has received numerous awards and accolades for his remarkable cricketing skills.
